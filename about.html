About Us

Founded in 2018?, the Leftovers Club is an initiative working to reduce food waste at UBCO. We provide free food whereabouts, access to community events, and useful nutrition/food tips.


Goals

  1. Reduce food waste
  2. Food sustainability, education, and awareness
  3. Teach you how to eat healthy on a budget
  4. Provide knowledge on freed food whereabouts
  5. Hold fun and educational community events
  6. Have involvement opportunities
